Task-3__What is Laptops

A laptop is a portable computer that consists of two main parts: a display and a keyboard. It is powered by a battery, allowing it to be used anywhere without needing a power outlet.

  1. Portability: Lightweight and compact for easy transport
  2. Variety: Available in different types for gaming, business, or general use.
  3. Battery Power:Can operate on battery power, making it usable without electricity.
  4. Efficiency:Modern laptops come with powerful processors, ample memory, and fast storage options, making them suitable for various tasks.
